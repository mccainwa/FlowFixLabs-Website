Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 28, 2025

FlowFix Labs (“we”, “us”, “our”) builds lightweight automations and internal tools. This Privacy Policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

Information we collect

  • Contact information (e.g., name, email) when you contact us.
  • OAuth data only as needed for the feature you request (for example, basic profile/email, file metadata, or spreadsheet content) and only with your explicit consent.
  • Usage/technical data (basic logs, error reports) to operate and improve our services.

How we use information

  • To provide, secure, and improve automations and tools you ask us to build.
  • To communicate with you about support, updates, and service notices.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

Data sharing

We don’t sell your data. We share only with service providers that help us operate (e.g., hosting, email) under appropriate confidentiality and security commitments, or when required by law.

Your choices

  • You can revoke Google access at any time via your Google Account permissions.
  • You may request access, correction, or deletion of your personal information by emailing hello@flowfixlabs.com.

Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect information. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

Data retention

We retain data only as long as necessary for the purposes above, then delete or de-identify it unless a longer retention is required by law or contract.
