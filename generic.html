About FlowFix Labs

Our mission is simple: help everyone—from solo professionals to enterprise teams—save time and money with personalized automations. We believe that whether you're running a one-person business or managing a large organization, you deserve tools that work as hard as you do.

Who We Help

  • Solo Professionals: Freelancers, consultants, and independent workers who need to maximize their productivity without breaking the bank.
  • Small Businesses: Growing teams that want to eliminate manual work and focus on what matters most—serving customers and growing revenue.
  • Medium Businesses: Established companies looking to scale operations efficiently with smart automation solutions.
  • Enterprise Teams: Large organizations needing robust, secure, and scalable automation infrastructure.

Why FlowFix Labs

  • Automation-first approach: We eliminate repetitive tasks with smart scripts and workflows before adding unnecessary complexity.
  • Practical AI integration: LLM-powered routing, summarization, data extraction, and de-duplication—always with human oversight options.
  • Fast, keepable solutions: We deliver in days, not months, with clean code, clear documentation, and full ownership transfer.
  • Works with your existing tools: Google Workspace, Microsoft 365, Slack, Notion, Airtable, Zapier/Make, AWS, and your custom APIs.
  • Transparent ROI: We define time-saved goals upfront and only iterate when the numbers prove the value.
  • No vendor lock-in: Portable Python/JavaScript code, simple schedulers, and your data stays completely yours.

Principles

  • Keep it simple. Start with the tools you already have.
  • Automate boring tasks; keep humans on decisions.
  • Prefer transparent scripts over black-box magic.

Approach

Scope a smallest-viable automation, ship a tiny v1 in days, measure time saved, and only scale the parts that move the needle.
